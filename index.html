About Me

I am an accomplished molecular biologist and computational scientist with extensive experience in single-cell RNA sequencing (scRNA-seq), spatial transcriptomics (Visium HD, Xenium), proteomics, and multi-omics data integration. I combine experimental molecular biology with bioinformatics and data science, leveraging R, Python, and cloud-based pipelines to uncover insights in diabetes, oncology, and neurobiology. My research spans pancreatic islet biology, biomarker discovery, and translational applications in precision medicine.
